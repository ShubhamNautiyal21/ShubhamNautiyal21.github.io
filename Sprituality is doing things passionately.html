Sprituality is doing things passionately

April 5, 2022

People often misconstrue spirituality as religion; a sense of knowledge is spirituality, rendering your inhibitions to embrace them with utmost honesty is spirituality. Words cannot express the depth of it, as it being an abstract can wrap itself around every worldly thing; but we are bewitched by the sparkly things that we almost lose its essence.

It is not only about abstaining oneself from the rapacity of wordly pleasure; it also mean to shush the outer noise so that one can fall in love with the inner euphonious sound. It could have many forms, yet it is formless, it is timeless, and it provides the utmost pleasure that could ever be experienced.

In order to awaken this, all we need to do is connect to the creator .We are blessed to have human consciousness, but still need to explore it to the fullest.
